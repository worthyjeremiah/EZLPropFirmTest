EZL Prop is a reputable prop trading firm dedicated to equipping traders with the necessary resources for trading in financial markets. As a prop trading firm, EZL Prop provides traders with capital, advanced technology, and a robust infrastructure to facilitate their trading activities.

To join EZL as a trader, you are required to complete an application process and successfully pass our evaluation. This evaluation process is designed to assess your trading skills and risk management abilities. By demonstrating your competence in these areas, you can qualify for access to our trading capital.

At EZL Prop, the trading capital available to each trader is determined based on their individual performance and risk management capabilities. We understand that traders have different needs and levels of experience, which is why we offer multiple funding levels to cater to various stages of a trader's career.

At EZL, we understand the importance of providing our traders with a regular and reliable income stream. To achieve this, we have established a bi-weekly payout system for our traders. This means that every two weeks, traders receive their share of profits based on their trading performance.
