Getting Started

EZL is a prop trading firm that provides traders with the capital, technology, and infrastructure needed to trade financial markets. We offer competitive payout structures, professional development programs, and a supportive community of traders.

To become a trader with EZL, you will need to apply and pass our evaluation process. This includes demonstrating your trading skills and risk management abilities. Once you pass the evaluation, you will be given access to our trading capital.

The amount of trading capital available to each trader varies depending on their performance and risk management. We offer multiple funding levels to accommodate traders at different stages of their career. Our funding levels range from $10,000 to $2,000,000.

At EZL, payouts are made bi-weekly. This means that traders receive their share of profits every two weeks based on their trading performance. We offer competitive payout structures that allow our traders to earn a significant portion of their profits. Our bi-weekly payouts provide our traders with a consistent income stream and help them to manage their cash flow effectively.

Once you sign up, we will send your logins within an hour. If you sign up on the weekend, we will send you the logins. If you don't find your email with the logins, please make sure to double-check your spam folder. If you still can't find the login email, please email our support at support@ezlpropfirm.com, and one of our team members will send you the logins asap.

Our website primarily accepts payments through credit/debit cards and Crypto payments using USDC - on the Polygon / Matic Network. If you choose to pay using Crypto, you will find all the information needed on the sign up form.
