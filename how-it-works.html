1-STEP EVALUATION

Start your evaluation process

We invite you to embark on your trading journey by participating in our 1-step evaluation process. This evaluation serves as a pathway to qualify for funding, wherein you can demonstrate your trading skills and risk management abilities. Throughout the evaluation, you will trade with a demo account, adhering to specific rules and objectives.

By successfully meeting our requirements during the evaluation, you will earn a fully funded trading account. This will provide you with the opportunity to trade with real capital and benefit from our scaling plan, which allows you to progressively increase your trading funds based on performance.

Don't let a lack of capital hinder your progress towards achieving your trading goals. Enroll in our evaluation process today and commence your journey towards becoming a funded trader!

  • 6% Max Drawdown
  • No daily drawdown!
  • 0 Minimum Trading Days
  • 0 Maximum Trading Days
FUNDED TRADER

Receive your funding!

Congratulations on showcasing your exceptional trading abilities as a TradingFunds Funded Trader! We are thrilled to extend an offer for a funded trading account, enabling you to continue trading and withdrawing profits while growing with us.

As a funded trader, you can request your first payout at any time, followed by bi-weekly payouts thereafter. Moreover, upon receiving your second payout, you will qualify for a joining refund, adding to the benefits of being part of our program. By meeting the objectives on your funded account, you can access additional trading capital through our dynamic scaling plan. It is worth noting that our program offers up to a 90% profit split and a maximum scaling limit of $2,000,000.

We firmly believe that this combination of factors, including funding, payout structure, and scaling opportunities, will provide you with the necessary resources and support to foster your growth as a trader.
